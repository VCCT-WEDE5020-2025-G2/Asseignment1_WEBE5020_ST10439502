Community Services at Hope Haven

At Hope Haven, we realize that being comfortable in a home environment is a huge need for some of our community. We offer two services tailored to support a happy and healthy life at home. With these flexible programs, we help you enjoy activities while receiving personal care assistance.

Community Living Support (CLS) Services

CLS Services support individuals living in their own or family home. This flexible program provides personal care assistance along with fun and engaging activities. Your Direct Support Professional may help with:

  • Personal hygiene
  • Light housework
  • Meal preparation
  • Transportation
  • Medical management
  • Money management
  • Finding new hobbies and interests
  • Meeting friends

Community Residential Alternative (CRA) Services

CRA Services are designed for individuals needing an intense level of support at home and throughout life. Participants live in homes managed and operated by Hope Haven. CRA services are individually planned and adaptable as you grow and change. Support includes:

  • Personal hygiene and healthcare
  • Behavioral support
  • Medical support
  • Total household care

Our goal is to ensure that every home feels safe, comfortable, and welcoming while providing the necessary support for a happy and fulfilling life.
